Upcoming Events

Blood Moon

A crimson, ominous moon that rises into the night sky, casting a sinister glow over the city of Yharnam and the surrounding areas. The Blood Moon signifies a time of heightened chaos, madness, and the unleashing of eldritch horrors.During the Blood Moon, the boundaries between the human world and the cosmic, otherworldly entities become blurred. The moon's appearance is associated with the awakening of Great Ones and the escalation of the bloodthirsty hunt.

Summon the One Reborn

A dark and grotesque process performed by the Healing Church and its followers which involving a series of sinister steps designed to bring this massive, eldritch abomination into the world, utilizing blood, sacrifice, and occult rites.
